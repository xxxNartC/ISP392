Branché Restaurant is pleased to introduce a special promotion called "Happy Friday". Every Friday evening, customers dining at our restaurant will receive an immediate 20% discount on the total bill. Additionally, for groups of 4 or more, we offer a complimentary dessert. This program is our way of expressing gratitude to our valued customers for their continued trust and support of Branché. Come and enjoy the cozy atmosphere and delicious dishes at an attractive discounted price. Branché Restaurant looks forward to welcoming you!
